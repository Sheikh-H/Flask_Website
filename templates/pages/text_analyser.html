Enter a piece of text into the form box below. This will then use a Python function to go on ahead and create a list of words with all the words inside the passage. It will then use some functions to derive different information like total word count, lexical word density, frequent words, and etc.


I had to use Google to tell me what 'Lexical Density' meant. Lexical density is the calculation of unique words in the text against the total words in the text. The term 'lexical' refers to the words used in a text, and lexical density measures the variety of those words compared to the total word count.


A High Lexical Density indicates the use of a wide variety of words and a Low Lexical Density indicates the use of repeated words or phrases. The more vocabulary you know, the more you improve your ability to write with a higher lexical density.
